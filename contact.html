Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical web design project take?
The timeline for a web design project varies based on complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality can take 6-12 weeks. We'll establish a clear timeline during our initial consultation.
What is your design process?
My design process involves several stages: discovery and research, planning and strategy, design and prototyping, development, testing, and finally launch. I prioritize collaboration and keep clients involved at every stage.
Do you provide website maintenance after launch?
Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and functioning optimally. These packages can include regular updates, security monitoring, and technical support.
Can you work with existing websites?
Absolutely! I frequently work with clients to redesign or improve existing websites. Whether you need a fresh look, enhanced functionality, or better performance, I can help transform your current site.
