Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical website project take? +
The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple brochure website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex e-commerce or custom web applications can take 6-12 weeks. I provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you provide website hosting? +
I can recommend reliable hosting providers based on your needs, and I offer managed hosting services as part of my maintenance packages. This includes regular backups, security monitoring, and performance optimization.
Can you work with my existing brand guidelines? +
Absolutely! I can seamlessly integrate your existing brand identity into the website design. If you don't have established guidelines, I can help develop a cohesive visual identity for your brand.
What happens after my website is launched? +
After launch, I provide training on how to manage your website content. I also offer ongoing maintenance packages to ensure your site remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally.
Do you offer e-commerce solutions? +
Yes, I develop custom e-commerce websites with secure payment processing, inventory management, and customer relationship features. I can integrate with popular platforms or build custom solutions tailored to your business needs.
